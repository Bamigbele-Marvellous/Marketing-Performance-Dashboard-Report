# Marketing-Performance-Dashboard-Report
### Project Overview
This project analyzes marketing performance for the year 2021 using a dashboard that tracks campaign effectiveness, marketing channels, audience targeting, and return on investment. The objective is to evaluate how marketing efforts translate into customer engagement, conversions, and overall profitability.

### Data Sources
The dataset includes marketing campaign data such as:
- Impressions
- Clicks
- Conversion rates
- Acquisition costs
- Return on investment (ROI)
- Marketing channels performance
- Target audience demographics
- Campaign types
These variables help measure the effectiveness of different marketing strategies.
### Tools used
The analysis and dashboard visualization were created using:
- Power BI
- Data visualization charts
- KPI indicators for performance monitoring
### Data Cleaning and Preparation
Before creating the dashboard, the data was prepared through the following steps:
- Checked for missing or inconsistent marketing records
- Standardized campaign channel names
- Aggregated impressions, clicks, and ROI metrics
- Organized audience segments by demographics
- Calculated performance metrics such as conversion rates and ROI
These steps ensured the dataset was clean and suitable for analysis.
### Exploratory Data Analysis
The dashboard analysis reveals the following insights:
- Total impressions reached 90.66 million, while clicks totaled 9.07 million.
- The conversion rate stands at 8%, indicating moderate marketing effectiveness.
- Google Ads generated the highest channel performance, followed by Facebook and Email campaigns.
- The Women aged 25–34 segment generated the highest marketing impressions and revenue.
- Acquisition cost remains significantly lower than ROI, indicating profitable campaigns.
### Results
The analysis shows that digital marketing channels contribute the majority of engagement and conversions. Targeted audience segments play a key role in campaign success, particularly among young adult demographics.
### Recommendations
- Increase investment in high-performing channels such as Google Ads and Email marketing.
- Develop more targeted campaigns for high-value audience segments (25–34 age group).
- Optimize lower-performing channels such as Instagram to improve engagement and conversions.
